About IJLES

An interdisciplinary platform for language, education, and society research.

The International Journal of Language, Education and Society provides an interdisciplinary platform for research examining the relationships among language, teaching, learning, culture, policy, identity, technology, and social life.

The journal welcomes theoretically grounded and methodologically rigorous studies that contribute to academic and professional discussions across educational and social contexts.

Call for Papers

IJLES welcomes manuscript submissions.

Submissions must be written in English and prepared according to APA 7. IJLES currently accepts manuscript submissions by email at ijlescontact@gmail.com.
