Indexing

Indexing and Archiving

IJLES will display indexing, archiving, DOI, and metadata information transparently after each service or record is confirmed.

Current Status

Current indexing information will be displayed transparently on the journal website when available. IJLES does not claim ISSN registration, DOI provision, Crossref membership, DOAJ indexing, Scopus indexing, Web of Science indexing, or other external database coverage until such information is confirmed.

Transparency Principle

Unconfirmed indexing applications, planned memberships, and future metadata services are not presented as active journal credentials.
