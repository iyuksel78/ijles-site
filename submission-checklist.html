For Authors

Submission Checklist

Use this checklist before submitting a manuscript to IJLES by email.

  • The manuscript is within the journal's scope.
  • The manuscript is written in English.
  • APA 7 has been followed.
  • The work is original.
  • The manuscript is not under review elsewhere.
  • All authors approved the submission.
  • Author information is provided on a separate title page.
  • The manuscript is prepared for anonymous review.
  • Ethics approval is reported where applicable.
  • Informed consent is reported where applicable.
  • Funding is declared.
  • Conflicts of interest are declared.
  • AI use is disclosed where applicable.
  • Data availability is reported where applicable.
  • Tables and figures are cited in the text.
  • References are complete and accurate.
  • All required files are attached.
  • The email subject follows the requested format.
