Call for Papers

The International Journal of Language, Education and Society welcomes original research, review papers, theoretical essays, book reviews, and practice-based studies that address language, education, and society from interdisciplinary perspectives.

Publication language: English Reference style: APA 7 Submission method: Email Review: Initial editorial screening and blind external peer review Reviewers: Normally three independent reviewers Fees: No current submission, processing, or publication fees Access: Open access Frequency: Biannual, two issues per year

Submission Address

Submit manuscripts by email to ijlescontact@gmail.com. Please read the author guidelines and submission checklist before sending files.
