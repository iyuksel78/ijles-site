For Authors

Author Guidelines

Prepare manuscripts for double-blind peer review and open-access publication in IJLES.

PrepareFormatSubmitReview

Before Submission

  • The manuscript must be original and not under review elsewhere.
  • The author file must include names, affiliations, ORCID IDs, and contact information.
  • The review file must be anonymized for double-blind peer review.
  • Ethical approval and consent information must be included when required.

Manuscript Format

Manuscripts should follow APA 7th edition style. Research articles should normally include title, abstract, keywords, introduction, method, findings, discussion, conclusion, references, and appendices where needed.

Submission Files

  • Blinded manuscript file
  • Title page with author information
  • Ethics statement and conflict of interest declaration
  • Supplementary files, tables, figures, or datasets if applicable

Review Process

All submissions are checked for scope, completeness, similarity, and ethical compliance. Suitable manuscripts are sent to reviewers through a double-blind review process. Editorial decisions may be accept, minor revision, major revision, resubmit for review, or reject.
