FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Short answers to common IJLES questions.

How do I submit a manuscript?

Use the online submission form. If file upload is not possible, use the email backup option on the Submit page.

Does IJLES charge an APC?

No. IJLES currently charges no submission, processing, or publication fees.

What is the publication language?

English.

Which referencing style is required?

APA 7.

How often is IJLES published?

Biannually, two issues per year.

Is every manuscript sent to reviewers?

No. Every submission first undergoes editorial screening.

How many reviewers evaluate a manuscript?

Normally three independent reviewers.

What peer-review model is used?

Blind external peer review.

Does submission acknowledgement mean acceptance?

No. It only confirms receipt.

Does IJLES guarantee a review time?

No fixed review time is guaranteed.

Is IJLES currently indexed?

Current indexing information will be displayed transparently on the journal website when available.
