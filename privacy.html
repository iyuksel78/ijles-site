Privacy Statement

IJLES uses personal information only for editorial and publication processes.

Because IJLES currently receives submissions through the online form and email backup, the journal may collect names, email addresses, affiliations, ORCID identifiers, manuscript files, reviewer reports, editorial correspondence, and ethical declarations.

Information is used for editorial screening, peer review, correspondence, ethical assessment, publication preparation, and record keeping. IJLES does not request unnecessary personal data and does not share information without authorization except where legal requirements or serious ethical investigation require appropriate disclosure.

Reviewer reports and editorial correspondence are treated as confidential editorial records.

Privacy questions may be sent to ijlescontact@gmail.com.
