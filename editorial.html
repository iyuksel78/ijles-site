Current Status

IJLES is currently establishing its editorial leadership and international editorial board. Appointments will be announced after the selection and confirmation process has been completed.

No editor names, institutional affiliations, or board roles are displayed until they have been confirmed.

Editorial Board Selection Principles

  • Academic expertise
  • Relevant publication record
  • Research integrity
  • Editorial experience
  • Disciplinary diversity
  • International representation
  • Commitment to ethical publishing
